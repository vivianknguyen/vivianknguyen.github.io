Reseat

RESEAT is a start-up tech company looking to change the furniture industry. They are creating a circular economy that will prevent many commercial furniture from ending up in landfill. As RESEAT’s Graphic Designer, I’ve designed: sales and marketing presentation decks; website landing pages; social media posts for Instagram, LinkedIn & Facebook (both static and motion graphics); and standardized branding templates for Reseat.
