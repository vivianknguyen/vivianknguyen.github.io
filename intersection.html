INTERSECTION

For this project, I went for more of an abstract representation for my drawings. This project focuses on intersection as functional and usable spaces. The site for this project is located near the BART Station and Sacramento Street in Berkeley, CA. This design incorporates a radial intersection. The central hub is where the main circulation is located. The spaces are organized so that the more public spaces are at the ground level, and the more private and intimate spaces are at the top. Since there are four masses surrounding the central hub, the space and main circulation feels tight and dark. The use of color brightens up the area, and it emphasizes the importance of the main circulation as a central hub.
