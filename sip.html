SIP

As the Lead Designer at SOMAmetrics, I’ve focused on helping brand & design their transition from a marketing B2B company to a tech company. Their product, SOMAmetrics Intelligent Prospecting (SIP) tool, is a project that I’ve designed from the start. As this is still an ongoing project, please note that the text and images are not from the existing application.


1. What is SIP?
A call guide tool to “...increase prospect connect rates, leading to more qualified appointments, resulting in more discovery calls that convert into sales pipeline.”


2. Who uses SIP?
Sales Development Representatives and inside sales reps.

Logo Design

The original logo design seemed to play more around the acronym of SIP rather than corresponding with the actual tool. I recreated the logo with a cleaner & simpler logo. The logo icon shows who would be using the tool.
